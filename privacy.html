Last Updated: July 29, 2025

California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") Notice at Collection

The following serves as our notice at collection ("Notice") of Personal Information. We collect personal information as detailed in this Notice and in our Privacy Policy below.

Categories of Personal Information collected:

  • Automatically collected data: This includes IP address, device type, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, geographic location (country only), and data concerning pages viewed and the time you spend on each page, collected by cookies and other tracking tools, when you interact with our Websites and/or Services.
  • Identifiers (including account credentials information): This includes your name, country, email address, phone number, username and password if you register an account with us. We may collect information from partners that verify your identity for account log-in purposes.
  • Communication information: This includes your contact information and the context of the communication (to the extent considered Personal Information) when you send us an email or request, such as name of workplace and job title.
  • Geolocation data: This means the device details, geolocation from where your device accesses our Websites and Services based on information like its IP address.
  • Network and services activity information: This includes content data and how you interact with our Websites and Services, including your answers to general questions (e.g., your preferred language, purpose of usage of the Services), and content supplied through your interaction with our models (e.g., prompts, text, documents, and any other content and information uploaded, entered, or otherwise transmitted by you in connection with your use of the Services), feedback, answers to surveys or other comments sent to us.

Purposes for which the Personal Information is used:

  • To provide you with the Services. We will use your Personal Information for the following purposes: (i) to allow you to create an account; and (ii) to administer and improve, enhance and develop our products and Services.
  • To allow you to make use of our Website. We will use your Personal Information to allow you to make use of our Websites, including: (i) if you complete a Website form, (ii) to answer your questions and to allow you to communicate with us; (iii) to analyze your use of our Websites and to improve our Websites; and (iv) to customize your experience.
  • For Administrative Purposes. Contestra uses your Personal Information (i) to respond to your questions, comments, and other requests, or information, including information about potential or future products and Services; (ii) to provide you with the Websites and the Services; (iii) for internal quality control purposes; and (iv) to generally administer the Websites and the Services.
  • To Market and develop Contestra products and Services. We use information to market Contestra products and Services. Such use includes (i) notifying you about offers and services that may be of interest to you; (ii) adding you to our mailing list, send newsletters or other marketing communications; (iii) advertising Contestra products and Services and other-related offers for you; (iv) conducting market research; (v) developing, enhancing, improving and marketing new products and Services; (vi) to measure interest in our Websites, products and services; (vii) other purposes disclosed at the time you provide information; and (viii) as you otherwise consent.
  • Security purposes. Some of the abovementioned Personal Information will be used: (i) for detecting, taking steps to prevent and prosecuting fraud or other illegal activity; (ii) To prevent fraud, prohibited or illegal activities and to enforce our contracts with you; (iii) to identify and repair errors; to conduct audits (including bias audit); (iv) for security purposes; and (v) to comply with legal obligations and to protect the rights, privacy, and safety of our users, ours and/or third parties. Personal Information may also be used to comply with applicable laws, with investigations performed by the relevant authorities, law enforcement purposes, and/or to exercise or defend legal claims.
  • De-identified and Aggregated Information Use. In certain cases, we may aggregate or de-identify your Personal Information and further use it, including, without limitation, to analyze and improve our Websites, as well as our products and Services (including through the use of artificial intelligence) and for research purposes.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies. We use cookies, analytic tools and other technologies to automatically collect information through, and to track users' movement in our Websites and the Services.

Sale or share of Personal Information: We do not "sell" or "share" Personal Data for cross-contextual behavioral advertising and we do not process Personal Information for "targeted advertising" purposes.

Retention periods: Your Personal Information will be stored until we proactively delete it, or you send a valid deletion request. Please note that in some circumstances we may store your Personal Information for longer periods of time. These circumstances include: (i) where we are required to do so, for example for compliance with legal and regulatory requirements in accordance with legal, regulatory, tax or accounting requirements, (ii) for us to have an accurate record of your dealings with us in the event of any complaints or challenges, (iii) if we reasonably believe there is a prospect of litigation relating to your Personal Information or dealings, and/or (iv) in the form of Aggregated Information (as described below).

In order to ensure transparency and give you more control over your Personal Information, this privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") governs how we, Contestra (together, "Contestra", "we", "our" or "us") use, collect and store Personal Data we collect or receive from or about you ("you") such as in the following use cases:

  1. When you make use of, or interact with, our websites (https://www.contestra.com/) ("Websites")
    1. When you contact us (e.g. customer support, need help, submit a request)
    2. When you subscribe to our distribution list(s) / newsletter(s)
  2. When you make use of the trust centers, documentation and status page (trust.contestra.com, status.contestra.com, and/or https://docs.contestra.com/) ("Trust Center")
  3. When you download, access and/or make use of, or interact with our application programming interfaces, or associated software, websites, tools, developer services, data, UI and design or documentation (collectively, the "Services")
    1. When you download and make use of the Services
    2. When you contact us
  4. When you interact with us on our social media profiles (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn)

We greatly respect your privacy, which is why we make every effort to provide a platform that would live up to the highest user privacy standards. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully, so you can fully understand our practices and your rights in relation to personal data.
